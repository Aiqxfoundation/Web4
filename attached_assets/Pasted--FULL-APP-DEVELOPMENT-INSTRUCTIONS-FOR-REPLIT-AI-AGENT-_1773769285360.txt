📌 FINAL (YOUR IDEA WRITTEN CLEARLY IN ENGLISH)

Create an application where users sign up using a username, password, confirm password, and a recovery question with an answer.

After creating an account, users must deposit USDT to activate their account. Deposits are manually approved by the admin within approximately 2 hours.

Once the account is activated, users can start mining Gems. For every $100 deposit, the user should receive a total of 10,000,000 Gems over 6 months.

Users can convert Gems into ETR tokens or USDT at any time. The conversion rate is:

10,000,000 Gems = 100 ETR

100 ETR = $350

The total supply of ETR is 21 million. 60% will be distributed to users through conversion, and 40% will be allocated for the team and liquidity.

The system includes a referral program:

15% reward from direct referrals

5% reward from indirect referrals (2 levels only)


If a user creates fake referrals, their account will be banned immediately.

There is also a dynamic rule: when more than 1 million ETR tokens are swapped, the conversion rate will change, increasing the cost of ETR in Gems.

The application should have a fantasy treasure-themed interface with a simple and clean design.

All deposits and withdrawals are manually handled by the admin panel.
